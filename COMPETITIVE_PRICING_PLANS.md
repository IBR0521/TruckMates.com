## 🎯 Why This Pricing?

### Competitive Analysis:
- **KeepTruckin**: $20-50/vehicle/month (expensive for large fleets)
- **Samsara**: $30-50/vehicle/month (enterprise pricing)
- **Fleetio**: $5-15/vehicle/month (basic features)
- **Your SaaS**: $29-99/month flat rate (better value!)

### Value Proposition:
1. **Flat-rate pricing** (not per-vehicle) = Better for growing fleets
2. **ELD integration included** in Professional+ = Saves $20-50/vehicle
3. **All-in-one solution** = No need for multiple tools
4. **7-day free trial** = Risk-free testing

## 💰 Revenue Model

### How It Works:
1. User signs up → 7-day free trial starts
2. User adds payment method (Visa/Mastercard)
3. After 7 days → Automatic subscription starts
4. Monthly charge → Money goes to your Stripe account
5. Automatic billing → Every month on same date
