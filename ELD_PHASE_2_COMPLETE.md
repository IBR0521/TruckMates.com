## 🚀 New Features Just Added

### 1. **Mobile Driver App** 📱
- **Page**: `/dashboard/eld/driver-app`
- **Features**:
  - Big, easy-to-read HOS status display
  - Current status indicator (driving, on-duty, off-duty, sleeper)
  - Remaining hours (big numbers, color-coded)
  - "Can Drive" / "Cannot Drive" status
  - Break requirement alerts
  - Today's hours breakdown
  - Quick actions
  - Auto-refresh every minute
  - Mobile-optimized design

**Perfect for**: Drivers checking their status on mobile devices

### 2. **AI-Powered Insights** 🤖
- **Page**: `/dashboard/eld/insights`
- **Features**:
  - **Violation Trend Analysis**: Detects increasing/decreasing patterns
  - **Driver Performance Comparison**: Identifies top performers and those needing attention
  - **Peak Violation Time Detection**: Finds when violations occur most
  - **Compliance Score Analysis**: Fleet-wide compliance assessment
  - **Efficiency Insights**: Route and speed analysis
  - **Driver-Specific Recommendations**: Personalized training suggestions
  - **Actionable Recommendations**: Specific steps to improve

**Perfect for**: Managers making data-driven decisions
