# Data Residency Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2024  
**Effective Date**: January 2024

## Overview

TruckMates is committed to transparency regarding where your data is stored and processed. This policy outlines our data residency practices and your options for data location.

---

## Primary Data Storage

### United States (Default)
**Location**: United States  
**Data Centers**: 
- Primary: AWS US-East (Virginia)
- Backup: AWS US-West (Oregon)

**Data Stored**:
- All customer data
- Application databases
- File storage (documents, images)
- Backup data

**Compliance**:
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- GDPR compliant (with appropriate safeguards)
- HIPAA-ready infrastructure

---

## Data Processing

### Processing Locations
- **United States**: Primary processing location
- **European Union**: For EU customers (optional, upon request)

### Third-Party Services
Some data may be processed by third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Location | Data Type |
|---------|---------|----------|-----------|
| Google Maps | Route optimization | United States | Route data, addresses |
| OpenAI | Document analysis | United States | Document images (temporary) |
| Resend | Email delivery | United States | Email addresses, content |
| Supabase | Database hosting | United States | All application data |

---

## Regional Options

### European Union (EU) Data Residency
**Available**: Upon request for Enterprise customers

**Features**:
- Data stored in EU data centers
- Processing within EU boundaries
- GDPR compliance guaranteed
- Additional compliance certifications

**Requirements**:
- Enterprise plan subscription
- Minimum 12-month commitment
- Additional setup fee may apply

**Contact**: enterprise@truckmates.com for EU data residency options

---

## Data Transfer

### International Transfers
- **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)**: Used for international data transfers
- **Adequacy Decisions**: We comply with EU adequacy decisions
- **Safeguards**: Technical and organizational measures in place

### Cross-Border Processing
- Data may be accessed by support teams globally
- All access is logged and audited
- Data remains encrypted in transit and at rest

---

## Data Backup and Replication

### Backup Locations
- **Primary Backup**: Same region as primary data
- **Secondary Backup**: Different region (disaster recovery)
- **Retention**: 30 days for standard backups, 90 days for enterprise

### Replication
- Real-time replication within primary region
- Asynchronous replication to backup region
- No cross-border replication without explicit consent

## Data Sovereignty

### Government Access
- We comply with valid legal requests
- We notify customers when legally permitted
- We require proper legal process (subpoenas, warrants)
- We do not provide backdoor access

### Data Localization Requirements
- We can accommodate country-specific requirements
- Contact us for custom data residency solutions
- Additional fees may apply for specialized requirements

## Your Rights

### Data Location Requests
- Request information about where your data is stored
- Request data to be moved to specific regions (Enterprise)
- Request data deletion (subject to legal requirements)

### Access and Portability
- Export your data in standard formats (CSV, JSON, Excel)
- Access your data through API
- Request data portability to another service
