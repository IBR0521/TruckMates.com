# How to Open Project in VS Code

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open VS Code
1. Open **Visual Studio Code** (VS Code)
   - If you don't have it, download from: https://code.visualstudio.com/

### Step 2: Open the Project Folder
1. In VS Code, click **File** → **Open Folder...**
   - Or press `Cmd+O` (Mac) or `Ctrl+O` (Windows)
2. Navigate to: `/Users/ibrohimrahmat/Desktop/logistics-saa-s-design (1)`
3. Click **Open**

### Step 3: You Should See
- Your project files in the left sidebar
- The folder name at the top: "logistics-saa-s-design (1)"

### Step 4: Push Your Changes
1. Look at the left sidebar for the **Source Control** icon (looks like a branch/fork)
   - Or press `Cmd+Shift+G` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Shift+G` (Windows)
2. You should see your commit: "Add employee management system..."
3. Click the **"..."** button (three dots) at the top
4. Click **"Push"**
5. Enter your GitHub credentials when asked
