## 🎉 All Mobile Improvements Implemented!

**Before:** Fixed `px-8` and `p-8` on all pages

**Updated Pages:**
- ✅ All dashboard pages
- ✅ All form pages (add/edit)
- ✅ All detail pages
- ✅ All accounting pages
- ✅ Settings page
- ✅ Dashboard layout header

### ✅ 2. Mobile Card Views for Tables
**Before:** Tables with horizontal scroll on mobile
**After:** Cards on mobile, tables on desktop

**Pages Updated:**
- ✅ Drivers page (table → cards on mobile)
- ✅ Employees page (table → cards on mobile)
- ✅ Invoices page (table → cards on mobile)
- ✅ Expenses page (table → cards on mobile)
- ✅ Settlements page (table → cards on mobile)

**How it works:**
- Desktop (≥768px): Shows table
- Mobile (<768px): Shows cards with all data

- ✅ All page headers
