# How to Paste in Terminal (Mac)

## Method 1: Keyboard Shortcut (Easiest)

1. **Copy your token** (Cmd+C)
2. **In Terminal, when it asks for password:**
   - Press **`Cmd+V`** to paste
   - Or right-click and select "Paste"

**Note:** When typing password in terminal, you won't see the characters (for security), but they're being entered!

---

## Method 2: Right-Click Menu

1. **Copy your token** (Cmd+C)
2. **In Terminal, when it asks for password:**
   - **Right-click** in the terminal window
   - Click **"Paste"**

---

## Method 3: Edit Menu

1. **Copy your token** (Cmd+C)
2. **In Terminal:**
   - Click **Edit** menu at the top
   - Click **"Paste"**

---

## Important Notes:

- ✅ **You won't see the password** as you type/paste (this is normal for security)
- ✅ **Just paste and press Enter** - it's working even though you can't see it
- ✅ **The token starts with `ghp_`** - make sure you copied the whole thing

---

## Step-by-Step:

1. **Run:** `git push origin main`
2. **Enter username:** Type your GitHub username, press Enter
3. **When it asks for password:**
   - **Copy your token** (Cmd+C)
   - **Paste in terminal** (Cmd+V or right-click → Paste)
   - **Press Enter** (you won't see anything, but it's working!)
