## 👤 User Experience

### What Users Do:

1. **Add ELD Device:**
   - Go to ELD Service → Add ELD Device
   - Enter:
     - Device Name
     - Serial Number
     - Provider (KeepTruckin/Samsara/Geotab)
     - **Provider Device ID** (from their provider)
     - **API Key** (from their provider)
     - **API Secret** (from their provider)
     - Link to Truck
   - Click "Save"

2. **That's It!** ✅

### What Happens Automatically:

1. ✅ **Device connects** to provider API
2. ✅ **Data syncs** every 15 minutes
3. ✅ **Shows everything** the ELD device provides:
   - **HOS Logs** (Hours of Service)
     - Driving time
     - On-duty time
     - Off-duty time
     - Sleeper berth time
   - **GPS Locations**
     - Real-time location
     - Historical routes
     - Speed tracking
     - Timestamps
   - **Events & Violations**
     - HOS violations
     - Speeding events
     - Hard braking
     - Hard acceleration
     - Device malfunctions
   - **Mileage Data**
     - Miles driven
     - Odometer readings
     - Engine hours
4. ✅ **Dashboard updates** automatically
5. ✅ **IFTA reports** use real ELD data
